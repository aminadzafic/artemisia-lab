Welcome to the website of ARTEMISIA Lab!

Founded by Dr. Ulya Bayram in Canakkale Onsekiz Mart University, Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering as a part of her Young Career Award (TUBITAK 3501), ARTEMISIA stands for Artificial Reasoning, Technological Exploration in Machine Intelligence & Systems for Integrated Advancements.

In our lab, we conduct cutting-edge research on various Data Science challenges including Computational Social Science and Biomedical Informatics. Beyond applying Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Complex Systems to solve data science problems, we also develop and advance novel methods and frameworks in these areas, pushing the boundaries of these technologies.

We are driven by two core motivations.
-First, to develop AI-driven solutions that address critical global challenges such as violence, mental health, climate change, and healthcare.
-Second, to actively support and promote the participation of women in Data Science and Computing, fostering diversity and inclusion in these fields.

To find out the story behind ARTEMISIA, please visit the About page.
