Our Master's Students Amina DZAFIC and Merve KAVUT Give an Impressive Presentation at the AI ​​Symposium

Our Master's students in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Amina Dzafic and Merve Kavut, successfully presented their work titled "Detection of Violence Against Women in Turkish Social Media Posts", which they carried out under the supervision of Dr. Lecturer Ulya Bayram, within scope of the International Cross-Domain AI Symposium organized by our faculty. This important presentation made at the symposium attracted attention to the innovative methods they carried out in the analysis of social media data in our department's Data Science Laboratory.


Dr. Lecturer Ulya Bayram made a presentation on developments in the field of artificial intelligence at the symposium. Our Electrical and Electronics Engineering department head Prof. Dr. Mustafa KURT, our undergraduate students, and other engineering faculty students also attended the symposium as listeners and had the opportunity to obtain comprehensive information about current developments in artificial intelligence and data science. Thanks to our speaker from Boston, United States, a rich learning environment was created.


We congratulate our students and their advisors, who contributed to the symposium with their work and wish them continued success.
